Welcome to
Coelho Construções

Dream Design Build Deliver

Coelho Construções, Lda. is a national construction company with Head office in Maputo, Mozambique and has strategic partners in South Africa. Established in 2017, Coelho Construções, Lda. operates as a one-stop-solution provider for all constructions, building and maintenance needs.

We operate and transact across multiple industry sectors. Our daily business operation focuses on the art of Construction, Warehousing, Air Conditioning, Steel Fabrication, Plumbing for individual, small and medium sized businesses to large companies and organizations.
